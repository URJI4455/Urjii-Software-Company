About Urjii Software Company

Transforming Ethiopian Businesses through Digital Excellence

Founded with a passion for digital innovation, Urjii Software Company is a premier technology firm specializing in modern web, mobile, and enterprise system development. We build scalable, user-focused solutions that empower businesses across Ethiopia.

The Founder & CEO

Urjii Abdurahman

Founder, CEO & Lead Developer

Urjii Software Company was officially founded on April 05, 2026. As a solo founder and full-stack developer, Urjii delivers hands-on, high-performance solutions using modern technologies like the MERN stack.

Clients benefit from direct communication, fast execution, and a strong commitment to quality and innovation.

Our Mission

To empower Ethiopian businesses with scalable digital solutions that drive efficiency, growth, and digital transformation.

Our Vision

To become Ethiopia’s leading software development hub, globally recognized for building innovative and world-class technological platforms.

Our Goals

  • Digitize local businesses
  • Build scalable platforms
  • Deliver world-class solutions

Future Expansion Plans

Urjii Software Company is on a rapid growth trajectory. While we currently focus on custom development services, our roadmap includes expanding into a full-scale digital ecosystem.

In the coming years, we plan to launch our own proprietary SaaS (Software as a Service) products, establish a tech academy to train the next generation of African developers, and expand our physical presence across multiple African tech hubs to better serve a global client base.
